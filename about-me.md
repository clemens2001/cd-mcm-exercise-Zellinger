**Why Continuous Delivery matters for agile teams:**
Continuous Delivery is essential for agile teams because it automates the release process, significantly reducing the friction, manual effort, and risk associated with deploying software. By ensuring that the codebase is always in a deployable state, teams can deliver new features and bug fixes to users much faster, allowing them to gather immediate feedback and quickly adapt to changing requirements without sacrificing stability.
